Easy tteokbokki recipe!!

Description

Tteokbokki or spicy korean rice cakes is a staple street and comfort food in Korean Culture! The basic recipe is made with rice cakes, fish cakes, and optional eggs. Here are the rest of the ingredients for this dish

Ingredients

  • Korean Rice cake
  • Anchovy Stock
  • Gochujang or Korean red pepper paste
  • Gochugaru or Korean red pepper powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Green Onion
  • Fish cake (optional)
  • Boiled egg (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. First we want to make the sauce. Mix the gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, sugar, and garlic together in a bowl and let them combine.
  2. Add the anchoby stock to water in a large, deep pan, and bring it to a boil. Once it boils, remove the stock packet and add the sauce that you made the previous step into the pot.
  3. Once the sauce is boiling, add in the rice cake and fish cakes
  4. As you let it cook, the sauce should thicken and the rice/fish cakes should become soft and chewy
  5. Next, add any optional ingredients you may want to add (eggs, cheese, noodles, etc.)
  6. Scoop into bowl and top with sesame seeds, green onions and sesame oil
