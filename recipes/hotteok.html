Easy tteokbokki recipe!!

Description

Hotteok is a flour dough pancake filled with sugar syrup inside. It is one of the most popular street snacks in korea!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • White sugar
  • Dry yeast
  • kosher salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • All purpose flour
  • Turbinado sugar or brown sugar
  • Cinammon powder
  • Walnuts or other nuts of your choosing

Cooking Instructions

  1. First we want to make the dough. In a bowl, combine the water, white sugar, yeast, salt, vegetable oil, and flour.
  2. Knead the dough/ingredients by hand or with a rice scoop
  3. Let the dough rise covered for an hour.
  4. As you let it cook, the sauce should thicken and the rice/fish cakes should become soft and chewy.
  5. Next, add any optional ingredients you may want to add (eggs, cheese, noodles, etc.)
  6. Scoop into bowl and top with sesame seeds, green onions and sesame oil
