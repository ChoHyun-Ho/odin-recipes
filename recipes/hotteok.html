Easy tteokbokki recipe!!

Description

Hotteok is a flour dough pancake filled with sugar syrup inside. It is one of the most popular street snacks in korea!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • White sugar
  • Dry yeast
  • kosher salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • All purpose flour
  • Turbinado sugar or brown sugar
  • Cinammon powder
  • Walnuts or other nuts of your choosing

Cooking Instructions

  1. First we want to make the dough. In a bowl, combine the water, white sugar, yeast, salt, vegetable oil, and flour.
  2. Knead the dough/ingredients by hand or with a rice scoop
  3. Let the dough rise covered for an hour.
  4. After an hour, knead the dough again and let it rise covered for another 20 minutes.
  5. Next is to make the filling for the dough!
  6. Mix the turbinado sugar, walnuts, and cinammon in a bowl.
  7. knead the dough one more time to get all the air bubbles out.
  8. Grab a palm size amount of dough, grab some of the filling and form a ball with the dough around the fillings.
  9. add some of the vegetable oil to a pan on medium-high heat.
  10. Place 1 ball onto the pan and let it cook for around 30 seconds.
  11. When the bottom of the dough ball is a light golden brown, flip it over and press it down with a spatula to make a thin wide circle (around the size of a CD)
  12. Continue cooking and flipping until the outside is a golden brown and the filling inside has completely melted!
